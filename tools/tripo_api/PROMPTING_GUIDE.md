# Tripo AI Prompting Guide for Cyberpunk ARPG Assets

## Character/NPC recipe
Use: `original`, `full body`, `neutral A-pose`, `clean silhouette`, `game-ready base mesh`, and clear clothing/accessory descriptions.
Avoid: famous characters, logos, readable text, fused weapons, complex hand gestures, and extreme poses.

## Enemy/robot recipe
Use: clear body plan, silhouette role, faction material language, light colors, and hard-surface shape language.
Avoid: named franchises, overly thin mechanical pieces, and too many small appendages.

## Prop recipe
Use: `separate object`, `game environment prop`, `compact silhouette`, `no readable text`, `no logo`, and intended gameplay purpose.

## Boss recipe
Use: large simple silhouette, modular components, optional segmented parts workflow, and strong readable weak-point/core language.

## Suggested negative prompt
`text, logo, watermark, copyrighted character, famous franchise, extra limbs, fused weapon, distorted anatomy, melted geometry, messy silhouette, unreadable clutter`

## Production reminder
Tripo output is a starting mesh. Finish scale, normals, retopology, collision, sockets, materials, LODs, and rigging in Blender/Godot.
